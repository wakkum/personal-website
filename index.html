About

How I work

Dutch investigative journalist based in Doha, six years inside Al Jazeera's Investigative Unit. I run the full range — from traditional source-based reporting and on-the-ground investigations to open-source work using satellite imagery, geolocation, vessel and aircraft tracking, and entity resolution across large and leaked datasets.

I publish in long-form text and documentary video, work solo and inside teams, and build custom editorial tools so one-person investigations can run at the scale of a small engineering team.

Always interested in good leads. Reach out.

OSINT Toolbox

  • Satellite imagery · time-series change detection
  • Geolocation & chronolocation of photos and video
  • Maritime tracking · AIS, port records
  • Aviation tracking · ADS-B, flight records
  • Link analysis across leaked datasets
  • Asset & corporate tracing across jurisdictions
  • Image & video verification
  • Document & file-metadata forensics
  • Custom tooling · scrapers, monitors, pipelines (AI-assisted)
  • Source protection · SecureDrop, E2E, OPSEC
